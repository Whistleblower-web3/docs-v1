### Innovation: Redefining the Anchor of "Value"

WikiTruth is not another DeFi protocol, nor is it an ordinary NFT marketplace. We are pioneering a new paradigm: **Assetizing "Social Truth" and enabling its monetization capabilities.**

![criminal buy](/docs/criminalBuy.jpg)

#### 1. Innovation at the Asset Level: Truth as an Asset

In traditional Web3, assets are usually tokens or artworks. WikiTruth defines **"Criminal Evidence"** and **"Social Truth"** as a high-liquidity crypto asset (Truth Box) for the first time.

- **From "Virtual" to "Real"**: Behind every Truth Box corresponds to key information in the real world that may change the situation. Its value does not come from sentiment, but from the rigid demand for truth in the real world.
- **Ownership Confirmation**: Using blockchain technology, we allow whistleblowers to truly own the ownership and disposal rights of evidence, rather than just being a "Deep Throat" who could be silenced at any time.

#### 2. Innovation in Economic Model: Delayed Disclosure Premium

This is WikiTruth's most disruptive design. We are not simply selling evidence, but designing a **"Time Game"**.

- **Taxing Evil**: Want to cover up the truth? Sure, please pay. We allow buyers (usually stakeholders) to purchase the right to delay disclosure by paying time fees.
- **Unsustainable Lies**: The price increases **exponentially** over time. This means that the cost of covering up the truth will eventually exceed the buyer's ability to pay.
- **Inevitable Disclosure**: This mechanism mathematically destines that: **All truth will eventually see the light of day, it's just a matter of time.** In this process, the vast majority of rewards flow to the provider of justice (the whistleblower).

#### 3. Innovation in Technology Fusion: Privacy Computing + Blockchain

We solve the natural contradiction between blockchain's "open transparency" and evidence's "need for confidentiality".

- **Oasis Sapphire TEE**: Using a Trusted Execution Environment to achieve "on-chain data invisibility, but logic execution". The contract can verify if the buyer has paid, but the buyer can only see the decryption key after paying.
- **Balance between Decentralization and Privacy**: We don't need a centralized server to keep evidence (preventing it from being bombed or seized), nor do we need to fully disclose evidence (preventing whistleblowers from losing leverage as soon as they publish).
