The original intention of Evidence Market was not to cater to the existing regulatory framework, but to solve a fundamental jurisprudential dilemma: **When judicial power is held by wrongdoers, how should justice exist?**

### 1. Monopoly of Power and the False Proposition of Compliance

In the logic of Evidence Market, blindly seeking "judicial compliance" is meaningless in some scenarios.

- **Justice is not absolute rightness**: If the people holding power are criminals themselves, they will never allow a project aimed at exposing the truth to be legalized. In this case, pursuing compliance is equivalent to compromising with evil.
- **The Mission of Web3**: The core value of Web3 technology lies in resisting the monopoly of centralized power. Evidence Market is a social engineering project about **censorship resistance, privacy, and anonymity**, not just a simple financial (DeFi) protocol. Its existence is to ensure that the truth can survive in any extreme environment.

### 2. Objectivity of Evidence vs. Legal Compliance

We believe that **evidence is an objectively existing fact, not an artificially defined rule.**

- **Facts are tamper-proof**: A criminal record, an audio recording, or a document—its property as the truth is based on physical reality and logic, and will not disappear due to the "non-recognition" of any judicial subject.
- **Censorship Resistance**: Using TEE privacy computing and decentralized storage, the project ensures that evidence is no longer subject to review, deletion, or modification by any centralized institution. The truth is no longer a "little girl to be dressed up," but hard, indelible encrypted data.

### 3. The Logical Paradox of Judicial Non-Recognition

Regarding the position that some jurisdictions may "not recognize Evidence Market evidence," there is an interesting logical paradox:

- **The emergence of the loophole**: Suppose Country A's law stipulates that "evidence disclosed on Evidence Market cannot be used as a basis for judgment."
- **Arbitrage by criminals**: If this is the case, the first thing criminals will do after committing a crime is to upload the evidence to the platform themselves, thereby using legal loopholes to obtain a "death-exemption plaque."
- **Collapse of national order**: If criminals can really escape legal sanctions in this way, social order will completely collapse, and the law will lose its meaning of existence.
- **The return of objective effect**: Therefore, even if the judicial subject subjectively does not recognize it, it cannot prevent objective evidence from playing its due role of deterrence and trial under society, public opinion, and the future rule of law framework.

### 4. Conclusion: The Truth is the Last Trump Card

Evidence Market does not seek survival through compromise. When the system strips away the cognitive superstition that "authority is always right" and shifts the standard of judgment from "artificial judicial compliance" to "tamper-proof objective evidence," it becomes a scalpel that cuts open the pus of reality. We believe that the pursuit of truth is a human instinct, and this is exactly what no judicial blockade can stop.
